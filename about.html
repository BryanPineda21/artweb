About Us

Baguettes is a modern art gallery based in New York City with inspired works from artists from New York. The museum opened in 2020, and its mission is to present unique pieces to the public and showcase elements from different artists. Baguettes collects, studies, conserves, and presents significant works of art across time and cultures in order to connect all people to creativity, knowledge, ideas, and one another.
